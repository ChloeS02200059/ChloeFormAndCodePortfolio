Project #5 Extermental Clocks

Description

The project for class 6

We are asked to design an experimental clock that expresses a novel way of tracking time. I found online that the waves on seashore doesn't just randomly come at the beach, but they come in a rythmetic cycle that roughly is 6 normal waves and one big wave, another 6 normal waves and one big wave. I thought this could be the recording of ocean time, in minuets (how many units of 7 waves there has been), and seconds(the number of waves there has been).

Design Process

I wished the clock to look simple and 2d to fit the p5.js drawing style, so I used small and big blue circles as the representation of waves, and controled the timing by using frame count to control the frame rate.

Reflections

I wished I could make smother animations in this programing. I still don't know much ways to present things like that in p5.js.
