Project #1 Lost in Translation

Description

The project for class 1

In this lesson we started with everyone drawing some pixel art together in an Exel table, and this week's homework requirement was to pick a pixel art out of everyone's drawings and make it into a program with p5.js

Design Process

The design of this drawing is by a classmate, it was made in Excel and I converted into a programed verion in p5.js. I also recreated the gray frame of the grids of the Excel.

Reflection

I enjoyed the process of making this drawing with p5.js. While completing this homework I learned how to adjust the weight and color of strokes, and also how to organize my codings with notes in between.
